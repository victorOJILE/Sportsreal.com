The information here is all about creating a new post body.
  1. Bold

    To enter a bold text:

    • Make sure the insertion point is at the right place.
    • Click on "B" button, a snippet will show up where you placed your insertion point.
    • Replace "Write here..." with your own value.
  1. Italic

    To enter an Italic text:

    • Make sure the insertion point is at the right place.
    • Click on " I " button, a snippet will show up where you placed your insertion point.
    • Replace "Write here..." with your own value.
  1. Underline

    To underline a text:

    • Make sure the insertion point is at the right place.
    • Click on " U " button, a snippet will show up where you placed your insertion point.
    • Replace "Write here..." with your own value.
  1. Hyperlink

    To insert a hyperlink:

    • Make sure the insertion point is at the right place.
    • Click on " H " button, a snippet will show up where you placed your insertion point.
    • Replace "Write or paste link here" with the url.
    • Finally, replace "Write here..." with your own value.
  1. Numbering (Ordered list)

    To enable automatic numbering:

    • Make sure the insertion point is at the right place.
    • Click on " 1 " button, a snippet will show up where you placed your insertion point.
    • Replace "Write here..." with your own value that should be on that list number.
    • To continue numbering, place your insertion point after "</li>" (no space) and click "Enter".
  1. Bulleting (Unordered list)

    To enable automatic bulleting:

    • Make sure the insertion point is at the right place.
    • Click on " . " button, a snippet will show up where you placed your insertion point.
    • Replace "Write here..." with your own value that should be on that list bullet.
    • To continue with bullets, place your insertion point after "</li>" (no space) and click "Enter".
  1. Quotes

    To insert quotes, e.g This text is quoted:

    • Make sure the insertion point is at the right place.
    • Click on " '' " button, a snippet will show up where you placed your insertion point.
    • Replace "Write here..." with your own value.
